Unit 9 · Remembering & Forgetting

Serial Position Effect

Principle: People remember the first (primacy) and last (recency) items in a sequence best. Middle items are forgotten.

→ Presentation Architecture

CRM must structure property tours so the BEST option is first, the SECOND BEST is last, and filler options sit in the middle. The primacy anchor sets the bar; the recency option gets the final cognitive processing.

34%
